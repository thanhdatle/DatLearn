Competitor Teardown Sheet

Twenty minutes, once a week, same day. Meta deletes commercial ads from the library the moment they're paused — so this sheet is the archive. Nobody else is keeping one for your category.

1 · Where to look

ArchiveUse it forCoverage limit
Meta Ad Library A competitor's complete live FB/IG set — creative, copy, start date, versions Active ads only. No performance data. No archive for commercial ads.
Google Ads Transparency Center UAC, YouTube, Search, Play. Up to 13 months back. No login. Verified advertisers only.
TikTok Creative Center Category hook patterns, global, with some metrics Curated — only ads past an engagement threshold appear.
TikTok Commercial Content Library A competitor's complete TikTok history EEA, Switzerland and UK only.

Always run one pass with the country set to an EU member state. DSA obligations mean EU-delivered ads keep a year of history after their last impression and carry targeting and demographic-reach data that no other region discloses. You will never buy Germany — look at it anyway. It is the only place the history lives.

2 · Triage rules

Days runningRead it asDo
< 14NoiseLog only. May be dead by Friday.
14–29Early candidateSwipe it. Don't copy the angle yet.
30–59Cleared their barExtract hook / promise / proof / CTA. Add to test queue.
60+Proven winnerUnderstand exactly why it works before rebuilding it.

Amplifiers: 3+ versions means they're actively iterating — protecting a winner, not leaving a leftover switched on. 3+ countries means the angle survives translation. Neither rescues a creative under two weeks old; longevity comes first.

One exception to the noise rule: a brand-new hook appearing across several competitors in the same week is a trend, not noise. Catch those early.

4 · For every survivor, answer four questions

The teardown proper — 30+ day creatives only

  • Hook. What happens in the first two seconds? Write the literal words or action.
  • Promise. What is the viewer being told they'll get?
  • Proof. What makes it credible — gameplay, a UI shot, a face, a number?
  • Ask. What is the call to action, and how early does it arrive?

Four lines per ad. If you can't fill them in, you haven't understood the ad well enough to learn anything from it.

5 · The rule that protects the ad account

Take the structure. Never the footage. Competitors running commercial ROM gameplay are not proof the practice is safe — the library shows only ads running right now, so banned accounts are invisible by construction. That's survivorship bias, and acting on it risks the channel rather than the ad. Rebuild every borrowed structure with µCity, Lan Master, Alter Ego and the app's own interface — the seed catalog is the only gameplay footage that is legally yours to show.
