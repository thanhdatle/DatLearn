The UA Maths Sheet

Every formula you need to decide whether to spend, keep spending, or stop. Print it and keep it beside the Ads Manager tab. If a number on your screen disagrees with a number on this page, the page is not automatically wrong.

1 · The chain

Active days(N) = 1 + Σt=1..N r(t)
LTV(N) = ARPDAU × Active days(N)
ROAS(N) = LTV(N) ÷ CPI
Max CPI = LTV(N) ÷ margin ratio r(t) = fraction of installs still active on day t. The "1 +" is install day.

Fitting the curve from three measured points, r(t) = A·t−b:

b = −slope of ln r against ln t   (least squares over D1, D7, D30)
A = exp(intercept) Two points fit exactly and tell you nothing. Three let the tail contradict the head — which is precisely when you learn the curve is lying.

3 · The decision rules

Phase 1 → Phase 2 gate — all three must hold

  • D1 ≥ 35%
  • D7 ≥ 12–15%
  • D7 cumulative ad revenue ≥ 25–30% of CPI

Fail any one → stop and fix retention or ad density. UA scales an economy; it does not create one.

SituationRule
CPI ≤ Max CPIBid. You clear your margin ratio.
Max CPI < CPI ≤ LTVBreak-even at best. You financed Meta for N days for nothing.
CPI > LTVStop. Volume multiplies the loss.

4 · Conversions you will need constantly

micros → dollars: getValueMicros() ÷ 1,000,000
eCPM → revenue per impression: eCPM ÷ 1000
ARPDAU from impressions: Σ (impressions/day × eCPM ÷ 1000)
Daily budget for learning: ≈ target events/day × target CPA AdMob reports revenue in micros. 5,000 micros = $0.005.

5 · Traps

  • Optimising on installs. The network finds people who tap Install and never return. Fix by wiring onPaidEvent → MMP → network.
  • Test-device revenue. Bidding sources return precision UNKNOWN, value 0. Your plumbing can only be validated on production traffic.
  • Editing during learning. Budget, targeting, bid-strategy and creative changes reset it. Decide, then leave it alone.
  • Reading D1 as retention. You need the area under the curve, not one point.
  • Chasing Tier-1 for revenue. Both sides of the fraction scale ~10×. Retention is the only lever that moves one side alone.
  • Commercial ROM footage in creative. Costs the ad account, not just the ad.
