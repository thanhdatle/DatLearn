The Two-Week Menu

Fourteen dinners, fourteen lunches, two Sundays, two shopping lists. The rules live in the slot grid; the options live in the dish bank; the fruit lives in the calendar. This page is the answer, already filled in. You do not have to think. You have to shop, and then cook.

How to read a day

1 active — exactly one dish tonight wants your hands and your judgement. Everything else runs on rails.
0 active — nothing tonight needs you. A reheat, a boil, a platter. These are not slack days; they are the days the system pays you back, and there are five of them in this fortnight.
Break — a restaurant-inspired dish that replaces the slot grid.
pit + quarter — this dessert contains nhãn, vải or chôm chôm. Not optional. See the box below.

The chip on each day — cá — is the anchor protein, the one in the món mặn. No two consecutive days share one. Check it yourself; that is the whole rotation.

The one rule here that is not about dinner

Nhãn, vải and chôm chôm: peel → split → remove the pit → quarter. Every time.

This fortnight serves them on six separate days — Wed 15, Tue 21, Wed 22, Thu 23, Mon 27, Tue 28. It is July, they are at peak, and they are also the classic Vietnamese paediatric choking emergency. A child's airway is ~10 mm; a whole longan is 17–20 mm, round and slippery — a near-perfect plug.CDC Vietnamese hospitals report recurring ER cases from hạt nhãn, including a 27-month-old resuscitated after swallowing one.case

  1. Peel.
  2. Split the flesh and take the pit out. Never hand over a peeled-but-whole fruit — the child pops it in and the pit follows.
  3. Quarter at 3–4. Halve lengthwise at 4–5.
  4. Seated, on a plate. Never walking, never laughing, never in front of a screen.

Every day below that serves one carries a pit + quarter marker. Full protocol, and the first-aid mistakes to avoid, in the fruit calendar.

Sunday 19 July — Batch-prep block 1

~90 minutes. It buys back four evenings.

1 · Cook the big pot. Cá nục kho — enough for Sunday dinner and Tuesday. Cool it properly before it goes in the fridge.
2 · Marinate and portion the week's proteins into single-meal bags: chicken thigh with sả + nghệ (→ Mon 20); anything for next week straight into the freezer. Marinated meat freezes better and tastes better — it absorbs the seasoning while it thaws.
3 · The sauce jar. Nước mắm + đường + tỏi + ớt, made once. A weeknight dish becomes "spoon two tablespoons" instead of "measure five things." Make a second, chilli-free jar for the child — that is their nước chấm for the whole fortnight, and it is what makes Wednesday's mắm nêm night painless.
4 · Greens: wash, spin dry, box with a paper towel to take up the moisture. Aromatics: peel and mince garlic and shallots in bulk; freeze in an ice-cube tray.
5 · Chia pudding — 3 tbsp chia : 1 cup milk. Stir, wait 10 min, stir again HARD. Keeps ~5 days → Monday-to-Friday breakfast, handled.
6 · Thaw tonight: nothing. Monday's chicken is fresh.

Week 2 · Wed 22 – Tue 28 July

Week two looks lazier, and it is lazier — only three of its seven dinners carry an active dish. That is not the plan degrading. That is the plan working: the batch pot, the platter, the one-pot, the reheat. If week two feels easier than week one, you have understood the course.

Sunday 26 July — Batch-prep block 2

~90 minutes, plus the oven doing its own thing

1 · Cook the big pot. Sườn rim mặn ngọt — Sunday dinner and Tuesday 28.
2 · Bake. Banana bread — and HALVE the brown sugar (to ⅓ cup, or even 2 tbsp). King Arthur explicitly blesses the cut; it stays moist and simply tastes more banana-y.src Double the batch and freeze one loaf. Omit the walnuts — whole nuts are a choking hazard at this age — or grind them fine.
3 · Marinate and portion: shrimp, peeled and deveined (→ Mon 27) · beef, sliced thin (→ Sat 25 if you did not already) · a spare bag of marinated chicken for the fortnight after.
4 · Top up both sauce jars — the adult one, and the chilli-free one for the child.
5 · Greens and aromatics: exactly as last Sunday.
6 · Chia pudding — Monday to Friday breakfast.
7 · Thaw tonight: Monday's shrimp, into the fridge. Cold running water over a sealed bag, or the microwave's defrost setting — never hot water. It part-cooks the outside and grows bacteria while the middle is still frozen.

The breakfast strip

You do not eat a planned breakfast every day, and this page will not pretend you do. Two things, occasionally. Both are made once and eaten several times, which is the only reason either survives a weekday morning.

Shopping

The shape of the market run

Proteins: once a week. Meat and fish survive the week once they are portioned and marinated and in the freezer. Leafy greens: near-daily, or every other day. They do not survive a week, and pretending otherwise is how you end up with a drawer full of slime.

Chợ Cồn is the default — a long stretch of fruit stalls, everyday prices, no tourist markup. Chợ đầu mối Hòa Cường (32 Lê Thanh Nghị) is the wholesale hub and genuinely cheaper, but peak activity is 03:00–05:00 — worth one early run in this fortnight, not two.src Chợ Hàn is tourist-facing; you will pay for the convenience.

The one thing this plan cannot do for you

It cannot check the child's protein. On the shared-fish nights — Wed 15, Sun 19, Thu 23 — the dish is sized correctly for the tray. A dietitian reviewing a real family-of-three tray found exactly this case: the fish was right for the table, but the child fell short once it was split three ways, because adults quietly moderate their own portion and nobody actively checks the child's.src Fry an extra egg. Boil a few quail eggs. Do not rely on their share of the communal plate.

And the standing rules, every night, without exception: take the child's portion out of the pot BEFORE the salt goes in — if it tastes right to you, it is already too salty for themsrc — chilli on the table and never in the pot, and end the meal at 30 minutes however little they ate.src
