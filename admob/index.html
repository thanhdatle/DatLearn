Mobile monetization

AdMob:
earn without losing users.

Mediation is the machine that makes ad networks compete for every impression — and the dial that decides whether ads pay off or drive people away. This course builds the mental model first, then the hands-on setup, for Android and iOS.

Lessons

  1. 01 Waterfall vs. Bidding

    The core mental model of mediation. ~6 min + one quiz.
  2. 02 eCPM, Match Rate & Latency

    The three numbers mediation trades against each other. ~6 min + one quiz.
  3. 03 Building a Mediation Group

    Assembling bidding + waterfall sources into one line-up. ~7 min + one quiz.
  4. 04 Ad formats & where they belong

    Interstitial, rewarded, app-open, banner — placement rules. (planned)
  5. 05 Frequency caps & the retention cost

    Where the revenue-vs-UX balance is actually won. (planned)
