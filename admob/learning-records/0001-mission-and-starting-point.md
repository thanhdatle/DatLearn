# Mission set: AdMob mediation & UX balance, two platforms

The learner chose AdMob with the mission of mastering **mediation and the revenue-vs-retention
balance** (not basic SDK setup), across **Android/Kotlin and iOS/Swift**. See [[MISSION.md]].

No prior AdMob knowledge has been demonstrated yet, so the zone of proximal development starts at
the **conceptual foundation**: waterfall vs. bidding. Lesson 0001 teaches exactly that. Future
lessons should not assume console familiarity or known eCPM/fill-rate numbers until the learner
demonstrates them — ask whether they have live apps with real data before teaching optimization.
